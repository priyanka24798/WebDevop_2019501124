Career option in Civil Services

The Civil Services refer to the career bureaucrats who are the permanent executive branch of the Republic of India. The Civil Service exam is one of the most prestigious career options.

Description

Civil servant is a government employee. This is a life time career choice. A Civil servant -on different posts- basically plays a major role in the progress and development of state or development of India.
